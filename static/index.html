Soil Quality Analysis and Crop Recommendation System

The Crop Recommendation System analyzes various factors such as soil health, weather patterns, and environmental conditions to suggest the most suitable crops for your farm. The system takes into account climate conditions, soil texture, and nutrient availability.

This feature evaluates the nutrient profile of the soil and suggests the most appropriate fertilizer to improve soil fertility. This feature helps support healthy plant growth.

Focuses on ensuring long-term soil health and reducing environmental impact. It uses data on nutrient levels to suggest sustainable farming practices that will maintain soil fertility while minimizing the use of chemical inputs.
